History
During the first 4,000 years of civilization, man believed earth was a flat disc surrounded by a dome barrier. Various religions, including judeo-christian ones held this belief. That all changed once Cappernicus had his theory of earth being round. Afterwards, religions began to adapt to this new level of reality. However, there is one hole in this theory… In the 1500’s, the technology to prove the earth round did not exist. So how did Cappernicus prove this?
Sailboats were the only thing able to travel over water, balloons were not invented till 1760. Ever since the globe model was introduced to the education system, it’s challengers have all but faded overtime. At least 20 generations of people from day one have been told that the earth round.
